Keyword Research & Listing Optimization: The Real Difference

This is where Helium 10 builds an insurmountable lead. Their keyword tools aren't just better — they're in a different category.

Helium 10 Cerebro is a reverse ASIN lookup tool that shows every keyword your competitors rank for, with search volume, organic rank, sponsored rank, and CPR (the number of sales needed to rank organically). We ran our three test products through both tools and compared keyword lists.

For our primary test product (a kitchen gadget doing $22K/month), Cerebro returned 847 relevant keywords. Jungle Scout's Keyword Scout returned 312. More importantly, Cerebro found 14 high-volume, low-competition keywords that JS missed entirely — keywords we subsequently targeted and ranked for within 6 weeks.

Scribbles, Helium 10's listing optimizer, is also uniquely powerful. It tracks keyword usage in real-time as you write your listing, ensuring you hit character limits while maximizing keyword coverage. Jungle Scout's Listing Builder is functional but doesn't approach this level of sophistication.

Ready to choose? Here's our recommendation.

After 90 days of real testing: use Helium 10 if you're actively selling and want to scale. Use Jungle Scout if you're still in product research mode or working with a tight budget.

Helium 10 vs Jungle Scout: Who Should Use Each Tool

Choose Helium 10 if you:

  • Already have a product and want to optimize listings and keywords
  • Run Amazon PPC and want automated campaign management
  • Want a full business dashboard (inventory, profits, reviews) in one place
  • Are doing $10K+/month and can leverage the advanced features
  • Want access to Freedom Ticket — the most comprehensive FBA training included with any tool

Choose Jungle Scout if you:

  • Are still in the product research and validation phase
  • Need supplier contacts and factory sourcing capabilities
  • Prefer a cleaner interface with less cognitive load
  • Are launching your first product with a limited budget
  • Run a wholesale or arbitrage model (their sourcing tools are superior)

The "both tools" strategy: Many 7-figure sellers use Jungle Scout for initial product validation (their database and supplier tools win here) and Helium 10 for everything post-launch — keywords, listing optimization, PPC, and profit tracking. If you can afford ~$170/month, this combination is arguably the strongest research stack available.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Helium 10 better than Jungle Scout?
It depends on where you are in your FBA journey. Helium 10 is better for sellers who need a complete all-in-one suite — especially for keyword research, listing optimization, and PPC management. Jungle Scout is better for beginners focused on product research and sourcing. For most active sellers doing $10K+/month, Helium 10 delivers more value.

Helium 10 Starter is $39/month, Platinum is $99/month, and Diamond is $279/month. Annual plans save approximately 25%. EcomNewsHQ readers can get 20% off their first 6 months through our affiliate link. The Platinum plan is what most serious sellers use.
How much does Jungle Scout cost in 2026?
Jungle Scout Basic is $49/month, Suite is $69/month, and Professional is $129/month. Annual plans offer significant savings — Basic drops to $29/month annually. The Suite plan is the most popular and includes all core research features.
Can I use both Helium 10 and Jungle Scout?
Yes, and many 7-figure sellers do.
