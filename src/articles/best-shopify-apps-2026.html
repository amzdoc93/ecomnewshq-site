How many Shopify apps should I install?
Optimal range: 8–15 apps. Each app adds JavaScript that loads on your storefront — too many slow page load speed, which hurts conversion rate. Every 100ms delay in page load time reduces conversion rate by approximately 1%. Audit apps quarterly and remove anything that can't demonstrate measurable ROI.
