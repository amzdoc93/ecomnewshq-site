Everything you need to know about Amazon seller reinstatement and our services.

General Questions

How long does Amazon account reinstatement take?
Most reinstatements take 7-21 days. Simple policy violations: 3-5 days. Complex cases (Section 3, multiple violations): 30-45 days. We focus on getting the first appeal right to minimize the timeline.
How much do your services cost?
Account reinstatement: $500-$3,000. IP complaint removal: $300-$1,500 per complaint. P-4 verification: $300-$800. Brand ungating: $200-$800. Category ungating: $150-$600. Free initial consultation for all services.
Do you guarantee reinstatement?
We don't guarantee reinstatement because Amazon makes the final decision. We guarantee thorough analysis, professional appeals, persistent follow-up, and transparent communication. If we can't help, we'll tell you honestly during the free consultation.
Can I appeal a suspension myself?
Yes, but each failed appeal makes the next harder. Amazon tracks attempts and may escalate to final review after multiple weak submissions. We recommend consulting before your first appeal for the best outcome.

About the Process

What is a Plan of Action (POA)?
A POA is the formal appeal document for Amazon. It must include: (1) Root cause — what went wrong, (2) Corrective actions — what you fixed, (3) Preventive measures — systems to prevent recurrence. Must be specific, factual, and address Amazon's exact concerns.
What documents do I need?
Varies by case. Common: suspension notification, invoices from authorized suppliers (within 365 days), brand authorization letters, business registration, Amazon correspondence. We provide a specific checklist during consultation.
What if Amazon denies my appeal?
We analyze the denial, revise the POA, and resubmit through escalation channels. Multiple appeal rounds are included at no additional charge. We also have access to alternative escalation paths beyond the standard process.
