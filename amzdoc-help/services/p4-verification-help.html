What Is Amazon P-4 Verification?

P-4 verification is Amazon's identity verification process designed to confirm the legitimacy of the person or business operating a seller account. It typically involves:

  • Submitting government-issued photo identification
  • Providing a recent bank statement or utility bill for address verification
  • Completing a live video call with an Amazon associate
  • Answering questions about your business and account details
  • Showing physical documents on camera for verification

When Is P-4 Verification Required?

Amazon may require P-4 verification in several situations:

  • New account registration — Amazon increasingly requires video verification for new seller accounts
  • Post-suspension reinstatement — as an additional verification step after account reactivation
  • Business information changes — when you update your legal entity, address, or bank account
  • Random security checks — Amazon periodically selects accounts for re-verification
  • Suspicious activity flags — VPN usage, login from unusual locations, or related account concerns

P-4 Verification Checklist

Make sure you have these documents ready before your video call:

  • Government-issued photo ID (passport, driver's license, or national ID card)
  • Bank statement from the last 90 days showing your name and address
  • Utility bill or credit card statement matching your registered address
  • Business registration documents (if selling as a business entity)
  • Credit/debit card on file — know the last 4 digits
  • Stable internet connection (minimum 5 Mbps upload speed)
  • Working webcam and microphone
  • Quiet, well-lit room with plain background

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Amazon P-4 verification?
A mandatory identity verification process involving document submission and a video call with an Amazon associate. It confirms the identity of the person or entity operating the seller account.
How do I prepare for the video call?
Have all documents ready (photo ID, bank statement, utility bill, business docs), test your camera/microphone, ensure stable internet, and prepare to answer basic questions about your business.
What happens if I fail?
Amazon typically allows rescheduling, but repeated failures can lead to permanent deactivation. Common failure reasons: document mismatches, poor video quality, or inconsistent answers.
How long does the P-4 process take?
The video call is 10-20 minutes. Total timeline from scheduling to verified status: 7-14 days with proper preparation.
How much does P-4 help cost?
