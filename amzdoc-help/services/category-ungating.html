Frequently Asked Questions

What Amazon categories are restricted?
Common restricted categories: Grocery, Health, Beauty, Topicals, Automotive, Fine Art, Watches, Jewelry, Music & DVD, Sports Collectibles, and certain Electronics and Toys subcategories.
How long does category ungating take?
3-14 days for standard categories. Categories requiring certifications or FDA documentation may take 2-4 weeks.
How much does it cost?
Starting at $150 for standard categories, $300-$600 for complex categories. Package deals available for multiple categories.
