What We Do

AMZDOC LLC is a professional consultancy focused exclusively on Amazon seller compliance and reinstatement. We help sellers navigate Amazon's most critical situations:

  • Account Reinstatement — Full account suspensions, deactivations, and policy violations
  • IP Complaint Resolution — Trademark, copyright, and patent dispute removal
  • Counterfeit Complaint Removal — The most serious type of Amazon violation
  • Section 3 Inauthentic Appeals — Account-level authenticity suspension appeals
  • P-4 Verification Support — Document preparation and video call coaching
  • Brand & Category Ungating — Access restricted products and categories

Our Approach

Deep Amazon Expertise

We understand how Amazon's Seller Performance team operates, what triggers automated reviews, and how to craft appeals that address their specific criteria. This isn't something you learn from a course — it comes from years of working hundreds of cases.

First-Attempt Focus

Every rejected appeal makes the next one harder. We invest the time upfront to get the first submission right: thorough root cause analysis, proper documentation, and precise language that speaks to Amazon's review criteria.

Transparent Communication

You'll know where your case stands at every step. No vague updates, no ghosting. We provide clear timelines, honest assessments of case difficulty, and direct access to the specialist handling your account.

Results-Driven Pricing

We charge based on case complexity, not hourly billing. You know the cost upfront before we start. If we can't help, we'll tell you honestly rather than taking your money for a case we don't believe we can win.
